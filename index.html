Welcome to Green Bites

Founded in 2010, Green Bites began as a small caf√© with a big dream: to make vegetarian food exciting, flavorful, and accessible to everyone. Our chefs combine fresh, locally-sourced ingredients with innovative techniques to create dishes that satisfy both vegetarians and meat-eaters alike.
